PUT YOUR VIDEO FILES HERE

1. Copy your MP4 video files to this directory
2. Example: video1.mp4, meme-wifi.mp4, spanish-slang.mp4
3. They will be served at: http://localhost:8000/videos/FILENAME.mp4

Supported formats:
- MP4 (recommended)
- WebM
- OGG

File naming tips:
- Use lowercase
- No spaces (use hyphens: my-video.mp4)
- Keep names short and descriptive
